Telco Customer Churn & Revenue Risk Analysis

An executive-facing Power BI dashboard built to make customer churn visible and actionable across 7,043 customer records. KPI scorecards, slicers, combo charts, and segmentation variables let stakeholders isolate exactly where revenue risk was concentrated, by contract type, service, and payment method.

The goal wasn't just to report the churn rate. It was to show which levers (contract length, payment method, service type) were driving it, so retention efforts could be targeted rather than generic.
